How do I earn more credits?
You can earn credits by watching ads. You can watch up to 5 ads per day to earn credits for cocktail suggestions.
How does the AI cocktail suggestions work?
Our AI analyzes your available ingredients and creates personalized cocktail recipes based on your preferences for alcoholic or non-alcoholic drinks.
I'm not receiving ads / Credits not updating
  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection
  • Close and reopen the app
  • Check if you've reached the daily limit (5 ads per day)
  • Ad availability may vary by region
Can I suggest ingredients that aren't in the list?
Currently, the app uses a curated list of cocktail ingredients for best results. We're working on expanding this list in future updates.
Is my data safe?
Yes, we only store minimal data necessary for app functionality. Your ingredient preferences and credits are stored securely, and we don't collect personal information.
